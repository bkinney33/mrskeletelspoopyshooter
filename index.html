Controls

  • A/left and D/right to move left and right
  • Space to shoot
  • Shift to increase player's movement speed
  • J for Jamie Key

How to play

The goal is to shoot every target on the level without running out of bullets. You start with 3 lives, and every level you fail will remove a life. Special blue targets will grant you a life when shot first on a level
